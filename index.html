ABOUT SPARTAN BOYZ

Spartan Boyz is a premier cricket team founded in 2017 with a vision to dominate the cricketing world with skill, discipline, and warrior spirit. Our team represents the perfect blend of youth and experience, with players who have proven their mettle in various domestic and international tournaments.

We believe in playing hard but fair cricket, where every player gives their 100% on the field. Our motto "Unleash the Warrior Within" reflects our approach to the game - fearless, aggressive, and always striving for excellence.

With multiple tournament wins under our belt and a growing fan base, Spartan Boyz is more than just a cricket team - it's a movement that inspires young cricketers to dream big and work hard to achieve those dreams.

ABOUT US

Team History

Spartan Boyz was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate cricketers who wanted to create a team that would stand out not just for its performance but also for its values. Starting from humble beginnings in local tournaments, we've grown to become one of the most respected teams in the circuit.

Our journey has been marked by perseverance, teamwork, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. From our first tournament win in 2012 to our recent championship in 2022, every milestone has been a testament to the hard work and dedication of our players and support staff.

Achievements

  • Champions - National T20 Tournament (2022)
  • Runners-up - Inter-State Championship (2021)
  • Fair Play Award - Premier League (2019, 2020)
  • Best Emerging Team - Cricket Association Awards (2015)
  • 3 players selected for National Camp (2018-2022)

Match Highlights

Spartan Boyz vs Thunder Strikers

Final

National T20 Tournament - June 12, 2022

Spartan Boyz: 189/5 (20) Thunder Strikers: 175/8 (20)

Jason Roy's explosive 78 off 42 balls and James Anderson's 3/22 helped Spartan Boyz clinch their first national title in a thrilling final.

Spartan Boyz vs Desert Warriors

Semi-Final

Inter-State Championship - April 28, 2021

Desert Warriors: 210/7 (50) Spartan Boyz: 211/6 (48.3)

A brilliant century by Ben Stokes (112*) guided Spartan Boyz to a memorable chase and a spot in the finals.

Spartan Boyz vs Northern Knights

Group Stage

Premier League - August 15, 2020

Spartan Boyz: 325/8 (50) Northern Knights: 150 all out (32.1)

A complete team performance with centuries from top order and a five-wicket haul by our spinners marked one of our most dominant victories.
